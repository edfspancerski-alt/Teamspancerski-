# Nutrition Guides

## Beginner Meal Plan
- **Breakfast**: Oatmeal with fruits and nuts
- **Lunch**: Grilled chicken salad with olive oil dressing
- **Dinner**: Baked salmon with steamed vegetables
- **Snacks**: Greek yogurt, mixed berries

## Intermediate Meal Plan
- **Breakfast**: Scrambled eggs with avocado toast
- **Lunch**: Quinoa bowl with grilled tofu and veggies
- **Dinner**: Grilled steak with sweet potato mash
- **Snacks**: Protein shake, almonds

## Advanced Meal Plan
- **Breakfast**: Protein pancakes with peanut butter
- **Lunch**: Brown rice with grilled chicken and broccoli
- **Dinner**: Grilled tuna steak with asparagus
- **Snacks**: Cottage cheese, walnuts
